allergens

PLEASE please let me know if you have any food allergies.

i handle common allergens in the kitchen as i bake a variety of things.
if i am informed, i'll know to take extra care.

i will not carry the liability in the case of any miscommunication.

enquire if you're unsure about your situation and we'll find a way around!
